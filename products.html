Product Tour

Absol DMS + eForms Software is a maven data and documents organizing architecture that saves your confidential files electronically for a lifetime. Make your office completely paperless by switching to our Centralized Digital Document Management System. Have a demo of the Signature attributes of Absolute DMS.

Dashboard

• Manage all saved documents in the portal from the dashboard.
• Get an overview of all ongoing operations of your documents, including upload and delete requests from users, as well as completed and incomplete uploads.
• View document analytics in the form of a bar graph showing documents' ratio folder-wise.

Filter Tab

• Use an intelligent file filtration system to quickly retrieve desired documents.
• Search department-wise by filtering client names and categories to get exact findings.
• Find professional blueprints in any repository by filtering out a particular category.
• Access a file instantly by searching relevant keywords or tags.

Add Documents

• Add or import new document files easily.
• Import documents directly from Excel or upload files existing in any directory.
• Seamlessly onboard any kind of pre-existing ledgers and electronic sheets.

Export Documents

• Export or download uploaded files and documents in multiple extensions or formats, such as xls or pdf.
• Get your document in the desired format with a single click.

Pending Documents

• Find all uploaded documents with incomplete relevant attachments separately in the Pending tab.
• Keep reminding yourself of their completion.
• Upgrade existing documents to make them more credible and easily findable.
• Attach supporting details to a particular document to exclude it from the "Pending Documents" section.

Completed Documents

• View all completely uploaded documents throughout the repositories in this section.
• See the number of revisions or attachments integrated with a distinct upload.

Edit Requests

• View all edits in documents requested by employees in the "Edit Requests" tab.
• Accept or decline requests made by employees.

Delete Requests

• View all requests to delete a particular file or document from any repository in the "Delete Requests" tab.
• Accept or decline requests made by employees.
