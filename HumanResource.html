Cope with Human Resource Documentation and Files

Dealing with the HR Department of the company is worth a task as its operations range from recruiting and onboarding new candidates to evaluating the performance of existing employees and also the organizational KPIs.

Your Priority should be people, not papers!

Save your work time by getting yourself rid of all the complex HR paperwork and documentation.

  • Invest the time on personal evaluation rather than gathering hard copies.
  • Smartly automate all the physical documents and forms through our DMS and eForms platform.
  • Divide the workflow - Assign hierarchal members with their particular documents
  • Quit repetitions - Automate all the repetitive procedures at once to save time.

Save confidential documents electronically in a more organized way.

  • Employees' personal details like home addresses, Medical reports, and bank account information
  • Easy and on-the-palm retrieval when needed
  • Highly secured centralized server

Pre-defined HR Solutions

  • Using our Human Resources management tools, automate the full hiring procedure of the new candidates electronically and get rid of papers.
  • With our eForms studio, design hiring forms using the drag and drop feature and assign them digitally to the respondents.
  • Design a candidate onboarding workflow and integrate it with the relevant electronic form by using our workflow studio.
  • Get those filled eForms emailed to your inbox with the integrated emails feature of our eForms system.
  • Manage the flow from our Workflow studio to direct different sections of the form to respective managers and ensure the hiring of the most suitable and deserving candidate.
  • By using our Document management system, Save all the details of the newly onboarded employee from day one to avoid future inconveniences.
  • Scan their Resumes, medical records, I-9 Forms, and Degrees without any hassle by using our Document scan mobile app.
  • With the 24/7 data serving functionality of our DMS, access your employee’s documents anytime and from anywhere with no waste-age time.
