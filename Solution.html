Document Scanning Capabilities of Absol DMS

The greatest document scanning solution provided by Absol DMS for small and medium-sized enterprises has the following key features, which may help you better comprehend its potential.

Optical Character Recognition

Scanned (image-based) documents that contain text can be recognized by optical character recognition (OCR) and turned into searchable PDFs.

Large-Scale Capture

Documents can be captured using a variety of methods using our document scanning and imaging solution, including batch scanning, scanning from email attachments and folders, and putting the documents in a paperless filing system.

Barcode Scanning

In addition to reading multiple barcodes, the smart barcode reader can decode multi-valued barcodes using delimiters, map them to document profile indexes, or utilize them to automatically name and file documents.

An organization can create, carry out, and automate a variety of business processes with the aid of a system called document workflow management. In these processes, tasks are handed from one participant to another for action in accordance with a set of procedural rules. You can also analyze process and performance metrics to see whether a process needs to be sped up or improved.

Many times, unnecessary and repetitive jobs reduce organizational productivity. These duties can be streamlined with the aid of a document workflow management system, which also ensures the follow-up of outstanding tasks throughout the business process cycle.

How Absol DMS Manages Workflows

The Workflow Management System of Absol DMS streamlines and automates key corporate operations including order processing, HR procedures, legal and regulatory compliance, modification requests, etc., and lessens the time and mistakes involved in manually circulating paper. With reusable, standardized workflows, it offers a uniform and transparent user experience.

• Productivity booster
• Document confidentiality insurance
• Transparency of processes
• Enhanced Customer Service

The process of handling contracts digitally, from their inception through renewal and termination, is known as paperless or electronic contract management.
The standard process for managing contracts is as follows: develop, collaborate, negotiate, sign, track, and either renew or terminate.
Online non-disclosure agreements (NDAs) or third-party contracts are frequent examples of electronic contract management that you can conveniently handle with Absol DMS+eForms Software.
The involved authorities may look for and examine contracts without having to manually locate and go through piles of paper thanks to vendor contract management software, which Digitalizes and archives all contracts into a central repository. With the use of Absol DMS+eForms, a regulatory compliance document management tool, you can automate the contract lifecycle in addition to managing all different forms of contracts.

Key features of our integrated e-Contract Management Software 

Centralized Repository for Contracts

Have a central database where all of your contracts are electronically maintained. Access to contracts and contract data should be rapid and simple for users. Manage users in accordance with their responsibilities and permissions to guarantee efficient contract collaboration. Provide suitable access to all of your contracts for your team from any location using any device, and make sure your data is secure.

Quick Contract Intake and Request

Stop submitting current contracts and requesting new ones by email. Create your editable web eForms (electronic forms) for contract requests that can be used to request or submit documents. With a few mouse clicks, send contract requests and save a significant amount of time and effort.

Integration of Smart Signatures
