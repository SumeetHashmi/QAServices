Dashboard:

Manage all the saved documents in the portal from the dashboard. The dashboard is a centralized look-up space for all the ongoing tasks and procedures related to your electronic documents. You can get an overview of all the ongoing operations of your documents as Upload and delete requests from the users as well as completed and incomplete or attachment deficient uploads as a prompt. Furthermore, You will always have a glimpse of document analytics in the form of a bar graph showing documents’ ratio folder-wise.

Filter tab:

An intelligent file filtration system to quickly retrieve desired documents. Search department-wise by filtering client names and categories to get exact findings. This is one of the hallmark features of our DMS as it capacitates you to find your professional blueprints in any repository by filtering out a particular category. As our DMS implements proper indexing to each document, hence it’s quite convenient to access a file instantly by searching relevant keywords or tags.

Add documents:

In Absolute DMS, Adding or importing new document files is a piece of cake. Import documents directly from excel or upload files existing in any directory. To avoid the hassle of manual data entry, our DMS enables users to seamlessly onboard any kind of pre-existing ledgers and electronic sheets. This will save you time that you may utilize in the up-gradation of a particular document despite re-writing it.

Export documents:

In addition to convenient document importing, coherent exporting of the uploaded document files is another feather in the cap of Absolute DMS. Quickly export or download your uploaded files and documents in multi extensions or formats i.e xls or pdf. Get rid of pro-download document conversions through third-party apps and avail your document in the desired format with a single click.

Pending documents:

Find all the uploaded documents with incomplete relevant attachments separately in the Pending tab to help you keep reminding their completion. This section will act as a prompt for you in the up-gradation of existing documents to make them more credible and easily findable. Once you will attach supporting details to a particular document, it will be excluded from the “Pending Documents” section.

Completed documents:

Once you will upload any file along with the attachments, it will be shown in the Completed tab on the Dashboard. This is the tab of your most credible and saturated documents stored a click away. Skim through the whole spectrum of completely uploaded documents throughout the repositories in this section. Plus, this section also shows the number of revisions or attachments integrated with a distinct upload.

Edit Requests:

On the admin panel, all the edits in documents requested by the employees will be shown in the “Edit Requests” tab, from where the requests will be accepted or declined. This section allows you the liberty to take charge of what’s uploaded to your database. As an admin, control the loop of documents mindfully to acquire the privilege of our Absolute Document Management System.

Delete Requests:

Similarly, if someone requests to delete a particular file or document from any repository, the request will be shown here. Being an admin, keep a keen eye on what kind of documents are on the verge of deletion. This will protect your dominant documents from unintentional removal or loss. Accept or decline the requests made by your company’s employees with a single click after making a decision.
