Privacy Policy

At Absolute Solutions, we are dedicated to safeguarding the privacy and confidentiality of the personal data of our users. This privacy statement describes the different types of data we gather, how we use and safeguard it, and the choices you have regarding the data you give us.

Information We Collect

The following sorts of information may be gathered when you use our services or visit our website: 1. Personal Information: When you voluntarily provide personal information to us through forms on our website, we may collect it. Examples include your name, email address, company name, phone number, and office address. 2. Cookies and Other Tracking Technologies: Similar to many websites, we may use cookies and other tracking mechanisms to gather data regarding your usage of our website. Your IP address, browser type, operating system, referring URLs, and the pages you visited may all be included in this data. Small files known as cookies are kept on your computer or mobile device. These cookies are manageable through your browser's settings.

Use of Information

The following are the ways we use the data we gather:
1. To deliver and enhance our services: We may use the data to handle your requests, respond to your questions, and improve your user experience.
2. To communicate with you: We may send you relevant updates, announcements or marketing materials using your contact information. Opting out of these communications is always an option.
3. To safeguard our rights and interests: We might use the data to spot and stop fraud, unauthorised access, or other nefarious activity.
4. To adhere to legal obligations: In order to abide by any applicable laws, rules, or legal proceedings, we may process your information.

Information Sharing and Disclosure

Your personal information is never traded, sold, rented, or given to any parties for marketing reasons. The following are some more ways we might divulge your information, though:
1. Service Providers: We could ask dependable outside parties to carry out specific tasks or offer certain services for us. Your personal information may be accessible to these service providers, but only to the degree required for them to provide their services.
2. Legal Requirements: If required by law or in response to legitimate demands from public authorities, we may share your information.

Data Security

We take reasonable security precautions to guard your personal data against loss, theft, misuse, and unauthorised access. However, there is no way to guarantee that any electronic storage or data transmission over the internet will be 100% safe. As a result, we are unable to ensure that your information is completely secure.

Your Rights and Choices

With regard to the personal information we gather from you, you have some choices and rights.
1. Access and Modification: You have the right to see and change the personal information that we have on you. Please get in touch with us if you think any information we may have on you is incorrect or missing.
2. Opt-Out: You can opt out of receiving our marketing messages by doing as instructed in the communication or by getting in touch with us.
3. Cookies: Through your browser's settings, you can manage cookie usage. Please be aware that deactivating cookies can change how you interact with our website.

Changes to this Privacy Policy

We might occasionally change our privacy statement. The "Last Updated" date at the top will serve as the effective date for any changes we make, which will be reported on this page.
